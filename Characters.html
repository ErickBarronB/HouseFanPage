Main Characters

Dr. Gregory House

Portrayed by: Hugh Laurie

Position: Head of Diagnostic Medicine

Specialty: Infectious Disease and Nephrology

Personality: Brilliant diagnostician with a sharp wit, cynical outlook, and addiction to Vicodin. Known for his unconventional methods and abrasive personality.

Background: Suffers from chronic leg pain from an infarction, which led to his addiction. Despite his flaws, he's the best diagnostician in the hospital.

Dr. James Wilson

Portrayed by: Robert Sean Leonard

Position: Head of Oncology

Specialty: Oncology

Personality: House's only true friend, compassionate, ethical, and often serves as House's moral compass.

Background: Despite their differences, Wilson and House share a deep friendship. Wilson often covers for House's mistakes and helps him navigate personal crises.

Dr. Lisa Cuddy

Portrayed by: Lisa Edelstein

Position: Dean of Medicine and Hospital Administrator

Specialty: Endocrinology

Personality: Strong-willed administrator who balances hospital politics with patient care. Has a complicated relationship with House.

Background: Often at odds with House due to his rule-breaking, but recognizes his value to the hospital. She's a single mother who adopted a child.

Dr. Eric Foreman

Portrayed by: Omar Epps

Position: Neurologist, later becomes House's boss

Specialty: Neurology

Personality: Intelligent and ambitious, often clashes with House but learns from him. Becomes more like House over time.

Background: Started as a fellow on House's team, eventually becomes the head of the department. Has a troubled past that sometimes affects his judgment.

Dr. Allison Cameron

Portrayed by: Jennifer Morrison

Position: Immunologist

Specialty: Immunology

Personality: Compassionate and idealistic, often represents the moral center of the team. Has romantic feelings for House.

Background: Lost her husband to cancer, which influences her approach to medicine. She's often the voice of empathy on the team.

Dr. Robert Chase

Portrayed by: Jesse Spencer

Position: Intensivist

Specialty: Intensive Care Medicine

Personality: Initially appears as a privileged young doctor, but proves himself capable and loyal to the team.

Background: Australian doctor who comes from a wealthy family. He develops a close friendship with Cameron and becomes more mature throughout the series.
